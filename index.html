About us:

The CSDynamo are a small, very friendly, very welcoming cycling club. We run a regular weekly club ride every Sunday at 9am, and ride an a nice enjoyable pace, taking in some of East Devon's best views and lesser known routes.

There will be a cafe stop for cake and coffee!

We also meet for a club social once a month to chat about all things (not just cycling).

History:

The “Cycle Sport Dynamos” was founded in 1984 by Mick Fagg & Ian Daveridge in a pub over a pint or three... It was a standing joke that the club was basically a bunch of drinkers with a cycling problem. The club name came from a certain Moscow football team.

The Utterly Butterleigh Audax:

The club has recently introduced the annual Utterly Butterleigh Audax, running in September it offers both a 45km and 100km route catering for all abilities, and serves as a great introduction to Audax riding.

Starting in Budleigh Salterton, you will head out to Tiverton via Broadclyst, then on to Gittisham and Honiton, before heading back to the start for cakes and cream tea!
